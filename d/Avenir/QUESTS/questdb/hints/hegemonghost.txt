A skeletal hands grabs you and pulls you through the orb!
Yanks you through stygian darkness and a misty labyrinth of white limestone tunnels.
Past galleries of bodies.... bones.... skulls.... 

...The dead are walking... ...marching... ...wandering....

Deeper into the shadowy catacombs, southerly, toward a room with bones stacked artfully against the walls. An ossuary. And at its center, a sarcophagus.
An agitated ghost is there. His tomb was robbed, disturbing his rest... 
If you help him, he may help you meet the Goddess of Death--without having to die.

Mist floods the room, blurring your vision. Always with the shadows and mist, that Sybarus...
