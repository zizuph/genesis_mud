The blanket carrier's emotes are:
bthumb............ suck your thumb
bhair............. when you are really scared
beyes............. when you are shocked amazed or scared
bwhimp............ whimper
bturn............. when you don't want to look at someone
bsob.............. stifle a sob
bblub............. blubber
bcover............ when you want to cry in private
bfetal............ get comfortable on your blankie/bag
bnose............. wipe your nose
bsniv............. snivel
bponder........... ponder something while being cute
bdrape............ when you want to be a superhero
braspberry........ Ewwww! Yuck! Just what it says.  You can also 
                    "brasp <player>" when you have a particular target in mind
bflex............. show your muscles
bpant............. catch your breath when you are tired
bheavy............ let someone know when you are overtaxed
                    do bheavy <obj> when a particular item is too heavy
bslow............. when you can't go any further (get someone to slow down)
btired............ when you can't go any further 
bcute............. when you need to charm someone you can do "bcute <player>"
                   or just use "bcute" when you need to be charming
bblink............ when you are really amazed by something
                    you can also "bblink <player>"
bhide............. hide behind someone
bpeer [adv]....... peer out when you are hiding behind someone
                    you can also "bpeer <player> [adj]" to peer out
                     at a particular individual
bclimb <player>... when you are really terrified it is best to head
                    for higher ground
bstare............ really get a good look at something
bankle............ hold on to someones ankles
bshow............. show off your blankie for others to see
                    you can also "bshow <player>" if you only want one to see
bkick............. when you are really mad kick someone
bstamp............ stamp your foot and let everyone know you are really 
                    frustrated --you can also "bstamp <player" when someone
                      in particular is the cause of your frustrations
