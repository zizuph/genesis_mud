========================================
FIRE KNIVES ABILITIES DESCRIPTION
========================================
Assassins have an array of tools in their arsenal which is refered to 
as the "Arts of Assassination". Most require sometimes crafted 
components, and most share same global cooldown.

An assassin at max rank can apply these simutaniously:

    *Envenom + One of these (Backstab, Eviscerate, Stun, Blind, Garotte or 
     Evade), which totals max 113 caid regardless of composition and combination.
     
    *Envenom requires the right coated alchemical weapon oil for the right
     kind of creature (else it will not fire). Oils are crafted by alchemy set 
     and a proper herb. The list of the following 
     craftable weapon oils are:
     --------------------------
     Manslayer      (humans and half-elves)
     Eldarbane      (elves, drows, half-elves etc)
     Dwarfbane      (dwarves)
     Gnomeslayer    (gnomes)
     Halflingbane   (kenders, hobbits, halflings)
     Giantsbane     (minotaurs, giants, ogres etc)
     Dragonbane     (dragons and dragonkind)
     Greenslayer    (goblins, orcs, uruks etc)
     Deathbane      (undeads)
     Naturebane     (wisps, efreets, elementals etc)


  Backstab
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
  Files: /d/Faerun/guilds/fire_knives/specials/backstab.c
  The Generic backstab available for all except for
  custom descriptors for added atmosphere and appearance, and
  sport a 90 caid when not tanking (and useless when tanking). 
  Opening caid is 169 is unseen by target.
  
    Combat Aid
    ----------
    90/169%
    
    Frequency
    ---------
    Backstab can be performed on average same as normal global variant. 
    
    Hit chance
    ----------
    Same as global variant.
    
    Penetration
    -----------
    Penetration is calculated using the person's strength and dex 
    weighted twice, and their wisdom. The backstab skill
    is also used. Of course, penetration also depends on the weapon's 
    pen.
    
    Everything else is calculated by the Genesis Specials system
  
  

    Envenom
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Files: /d/Faerun/guilds/fire_knives/specials/envenom.c
    Quick left hand dps special. Requires a wielded dagger or
    knife in the left hand that is coated with a alchemical weapon 
    oil.
 
    *This special shares NO global cooldown with other guild
     specials.
    
    Rank-locked
    -----------
    Yes. Requires x promoted only rank to unlock.
    
    Required Ingredient
    -------------------
    Dagger with alchemical weapon oil applied.
    
    Combat Aid
    ----------
    33%
    
    Frequency
    ---------
    Eviscerate can be performed on average once every 13 seconds. There
    is a 8 second preparation time, followed by a 5 second cooldown
    time. It uses the Genesis Specials, so the timings of these are
    randomized.
    
    Hit chance
    ----------
    Tohit is set to be 95. This is calculated to be 84% hit chance
    according to 'man cb_tohit'.
    
    Penetration
    -----------
    Penetration is calculated using the person's strength and dex 
    weighted twice, and their wisdom. The art of assassination skill
    is also used. Penetration also depends on the weapon's 
    pen.

  Blind
  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
    Files: /d/Faerun/guilds/fire_knives/specials/blind.c
    Quick action using an alchemical compound (blinding powder) that 
    affects the eyes of most living creatures through searing
    pain and irritation.
    
    *This special shares same global cooldown with all other guild
     specials except envenom.
     
     
    Rank-locked
    -----------
    Yes. Requires x promoted only rank to unlock.
    
    Required Ingredient
    -------------------
    Blinding powder.
    
    Combat Aid
    ----------
    90%
    
    Frequency
    ---------
    Blind can be performed on average once every 30 seconds. There
    is a 2 second preparation time, followed by a 28 second cooldown
    time. It uses the Genesis Specials, so the timings of these are
    randomized.
    
    Hit chance
    ----------
    task = actor->resolve_task(TASK_ROUTINE, 
           ({ SKILL_AVG, TS_OCC, TS_WIS, SKILL_END, SS_ART }),
           enemy, ({ SKILL_AVG, TS_DIS, TS_INT, SKILL_END }));
           
    task = min(task, 100) / 3;

    If task is larger than 0, then blinding powder hits.    
    
    Penetration
    -----------
    N/A
