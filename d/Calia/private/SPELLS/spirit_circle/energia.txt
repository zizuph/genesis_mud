Energy Drain -     This spell will drain hit points (life force) from
                   an enemy and transfer a portion of them back to 
                   the caster. The amount drained and the amount 
                   returned back to the caster will be determined by
                   guildstat, spell skills and target resistance. This
                   will initiate combat and can be cast during combat.
                   Requires a pre-made component (can only be made in
                   the guildhome) consisting of sapphire and sea bubble.
                   175 mana. Max drain is 50% of the target's HPs, 50% 
                   of that amount are given back to the caster.
                   This spell is available level 7 members (Master) and 
                   above.
