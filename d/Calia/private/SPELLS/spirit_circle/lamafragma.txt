Blade Barrier -    This spell will place a barrier of blades on an exit
                   to a room. This barrier will be passable, but all 
                   those who do will take damage,including the caster.
                   The damage will depend upon the guild_stat of the
                   caster as well as the spell skills of the caster. 
                   This spell will not initiate combat and can be 
                   cast in combat. Requires a pre-made component (can
                   only be made in guildhome) consisting of onyx and 
                   bloodweed. 75 mana. Lasts 60 to 180 seconds. Does
                   50 to 400 HPs damage. If player trying to go through
                   has magic resistance of higher than 40, no damage.
                   This spell is available to all level 3 Master members
                   (guild level 6) and higher.
