Name Forget -      This spell will erase the memory of the caster's 
                   name from the target, causing them to no longer
                   "know" the caster and to have forgotten them. This
                   will not initiate combat. It will not be able to be
                   cast in combat. Requires an amber. 25 mana. This spell
                   is available to all level 2 guild members and above.
