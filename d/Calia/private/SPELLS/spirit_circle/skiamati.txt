Shadow Eyes -      This will allow the caster to see in the dark and
                   possibly even have the ability to see invisible
                   people. The chances of it allowing the player to
                   see invisible players will depend on their guildstat
                   and spell skills. It will not initiate combat and
                   cannot be cast in combat. Requires amethyst. 70
                   mana. 20% chance to see invisible. Max time is 600. 
                   This spell is available to all level 2 Master members 
                   (guild level 5) and higher.
