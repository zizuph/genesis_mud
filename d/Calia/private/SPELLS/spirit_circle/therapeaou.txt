Cure Wounds -      This will be a fairly powerful healing spell. The 
                   amount healed will depend on guild_stat and spell
                   skills. This spell will be able to be cast upon
                   themselves, or others that they choose to heal 
                   (including npcs if they so choose). It may be cast
                   in combat. It will not initiate combat. Requires a
                   pearl. 100 mana. Min healed is 100, max is 600. It
                   has a 1 in 1000 chance of doing a critical heal (max
                   is 1200 pts). This spell is available to all level 
                   1 Master members (guild level 4) and higher.
