Spiritual Hammer - This spell will cause damage to the target with a maximum
                   pen of 325 (minimum 75) unless the target is undead, then
                   the maximum pen is 423 (30% bonus). Requires a sfyri component
                   (made from a garnet, heart and a weapon). 50 mana. 
                   This spell is available to all level 3 Master members (guild 
                   level 6) and higher guild members.
