Peace -            This will stop the fighting in a room. If the enemies
                   are auto-attackers (aggressive), they may re-attack
                   immediately if they wish to do so. This spell will be
                   able to be cast in combat (obviously) and will NOT
                   initiate combat (obviously again). Requires a
                   carnelian. 40 mana. This spell is available to all
                   level 3 and higher guild members.
