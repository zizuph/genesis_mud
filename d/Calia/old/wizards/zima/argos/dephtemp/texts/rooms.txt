***************************************************************************
**        Descriptions of rooms in DEPHTEMP area  - see map for refrence **
***************************************************************************
NOTES:
   rooms: all found in ~zima/argos/dephtemp/rooms
   obj:   all found in ~zima/argos/dephtemp/objects
   npc:   the monsters in the room, found in ~zima/argos/dephtemp/monsters
   quest: location of quest objects and hints
 
ZIMA/ARGOS/NWTERR/ROOMS/FOLD.C - sheep fold where lamb is found
              npc: Lamb (NWTERR/MONSTERS/LAMB.C) can only be attacked if
                      attacker is wearing robe/medallion and wielding
                      the sacred dagger.
                   Lamb's corpse needed to do quest.
              obj: LAMBBODY.C - this "corpse" replaces the regular corpse
                   and does not decay. needed for quest.
 
ANTERME.C   - Eastern anteroom of sanctuary - a meeting hall
              quest: Delta tome found here
              obj:   TOMED.C found with 'take tome from pulpit'
 
ANTERMW.C   - Western anteroom of sanctuary - a meeting hall
              quest: Gamma tome found here
              obj:   TOMEG.C found with 'take tome from pulpit'
 
ARMOURY.C   - Armoury/forge of the temple
              obj: FOHAMMER.C, SWORD.C - just found laying on anvil
                   (free loot).
 
CAPTNGRD.C  - Office of the captain of the guard
              npc:   CAPTNGRD.C
              quest: 8th scene/prayer found on tapestry.
              obj:   CPTNSEAL.C - the Captain gives these to anyone who
                                  requests them
              The player may 'request permission from captain' here and
              receive the captain's seal; with the seal, the TOWGUARDs
              in STAIR1/2 will allow the player to pass.
 
CHAPEL.C    - Prayer Chapel of the temple
              quest: If the player is wearing robe/medallion and is poisoned
                        by the sacramental wine which has received the
                        "blessing of the talisman", the player can
                        'pray for revelation' and get the text of the
                        scene he must find in the artwork of the temple.
                     If the player is wearing robe/medallion and is poisoned,
                        he can 'pray <prayer assigned to scene>' here and
                        receive the 'blessing of being chosen', indicated by
                        the invisible object VDQBLESS.C
              special: To pray properly, the player must 'kneel before altar'
                       which gives him the VDQKNEEL.C invisible object.
                       The player can not go west (out of room) until he
                       does 'rise from meditation', which removes the
                       invisible kneeling object. This instruction is
                       given if the player tries to exit while kneeling.
              obj: VISKNEEL.C (invis) - given at   'kneel before altar'
                                        removed at 'rise from meditation'
                                        required to do prayers
                   VISBLESS.C (invis) - given when player prays the correct
                                        prayer assigned to him.
                                        required to take 'take dagger from
                                        pedestal' in SACRIS.C
 
CLASSRM.C   - Classroom for children and novice priests
              quest: - Beta tome found on poteum
                     - Plaque on wall lists letters of Greek (Argosian)
                       alphabet in order. This is the key to reading the
                       seven tomes in order, as they are each marked by
                       a letter of the Greek alphabet (alpha tome, etc)
              obj:   TOMEB.C
 
CORRxy.C    - Corridor rooms through the temple, x=level, y=corr room #
              12, 14, 16, 18: npc:   CORGUARD.C (1)
              26:             quest: Sculpture with 13th scene/prayer
              28:             quest: Sculpture with 7th  scene/prayer
 
COURT.C     - Courtyard in front of temple
 
DINE1.C     - Dining Room (north)
              quest: 6th scene/prayer found on mural here
              obj:   DBOWL.C, DGOBLET.C - just some loot that can be
                        taken with 'take bowl/goblet from table'. These
                        "dirty" objects can then be taken to KITCHEN.C
                        to be washed, increasing their value.
 
DINE2.C     - Dining Room (south)
              quest: 3rd scene/prayer found on mural here
 
FOYER.C     - Foyer (Entrance room) of the temple
 
HIERCHAM.C  - Hierophant's chamber
              npc:   HIERPHNT.C - The leader of the Order
                     ATKRAVEN.C - 6 of these appear when the Hierophant
                                  is attacked and she casts a spell to
                                  create them. They auto-attack.
              quest: the robe is found here
              obj:   VISROBE.C found in wardrobe; do 'take robe from
                        wardrobe' to get it.
 
KITCHEN.C   - Kitchen of the temple; food being prepared
              npc:     OLDSERV.C, YUNGSERV.C - "good aligned"
              special: If player finds dirty bowl (objects/dbowl) and/or
                       dirty goblet (objects/dgoblet) in the dining room
                       (DINE1.C), he can 'wash bowl' and 'wash goblet'
                       here which cleans them up and increases the value
                       of the objects 5 times. (nothing to do with quest,
                       just something to do to get loot).
 
LIBRARY1.C  - Front room of library
              npc:   LIBRARYN.C
              quest: The librarian gives hints to read books in temple
                     for "wisdom"
 
LIBRARY2.C  - Central room of library
              quest: books on shelves can be read to give history of the
                     Order, which are in turn hints for the quest.
 
LIBRARY3.C  - Back room of the library - room where sacred scrolls are kept
              quest: 2 of the 7 scrolls are found here, Alpha (first) and
                     Eta (last).
              obj:   TOMEA.C, TOMEE.C - 'take tome(s) from altar'
 
LOUNGE.C    - Lounge/rec-room of temple
              npc: ELDERPST.C (2) - two old priests playing a chess-like game
                                    to kill for loot
              quest: Epsilon tome found here
                     5th Scene/prayer found here on mural
              obj:   TOMEE.C - 'take tome from desk'
 
MASTRNOV.C  - Office of the Master of Novices
              npc:   MASTRNOV.C
              quest: Fourth scene/prayer found on tapestry
 
QRTPRN.C &  - Priests' quarters.
QRTPRS.C      npc: QRTPRST.C (3) - someones to kill for loot
 
QRTPRW.C    - Training room
              npc: PRACPRST.C (2) - young priests practicing/training
 
QRTPSE.C    - Priestess' quarters, bathing pool
              npc: BATHPRST.C - to kill for loot.
              has some artwork, but none helps for quest :(
 
QRTPSN.C    - Priestess' quarters
              npc:   QRTPRSTS.C (3) - someones to kill for loot
              quest: Zeta Tome found here
              obj:   TOMEZ.C - with 'take tome from desk'
QRTPSS.C    - Priestess' quarters
              npc:   QRTPRSTS.C (3) - someones to kill for loot
 
QUARTNPR.C  - Novice Priests' quarters
              npc:   NOVPRST.C (3) - lower levels to kill for loot
              quest: 10th scene/prayer found here
 
QUARTNPS.C  - Novice Priestess' quarters
              npc:   NOVPRSTS.C (3) - lower levels to kill for loot
              quest: 11th scene/prayer found here
 
QUARTCH.C   - Children's quarters
              npc:   CHILD.C (4) - little someones to kill for loot
              quest: 1rst scene/prayer found on picture here
 
QUARTSLV.C  - Servants' quarters
              npc: ASERVANT.C (3) - "good aligned"
 
RECEPT.C    - Reception chamber of the temple; a meeting of "rebel allies"
              is forming here. This is why the Dephonians aren't "too"
              upset about outsiders wandering about--they are expecting guests.
              npc: RECKRETN.C - representative of Kretos
                   RECNOBLE.C - representative of rebles inside city of Argos
                   RECPRSTS.C - hostess for meeting
              obj: BACHLAVA.C (8) - refreshments (pastries); found on tray
                   OUZO.C     (4) - refreshments (beerlike); found on tray
              quest: 9th scene/prayer found here.
 
PORTICO.C   - Portico (front porch) of the temple
              npc:   PORGUARD.C (3)
              quest: The 12th scene/prayer found on ceiling here
 
SACRIS.C    - Sacristy of the temple - where sacred objects are kept
              npc:   SACRIS.C - the sacristan, gives some quest hints
              quest: This is where the 3 sacred objects needed to do the
                     quest are found, each on one of three pedestals in
                     the room.
              obj:   ARMOURS/VISMEDAL.C - medallion of dephonia
                        player must wear this in all parts of the quest
                        player must wear the hierophant's robe
                           (armours/visrobe.c) found in her chambers
                           (HIERCHAM.C) to 'take medallion from pedestal'
                     WEAPONS/VISDAGGR.C - sacred dagger
                        needed to kill the 'innocent' lamb
                           (~zima/nwterr/monsters/lamb.c) found in the fold
                           (~zima/nwterr/rooms/fold.c)
                        player must wear robe/medallion and be blessed in
                           chapel (have VDQBLESS.C invis obj) to
                           'take dagger from pedestal'
                     VISBOWL.C - ritualistic bowl to hold blood of the lamb
                        needed to do final rite in SANCT.C
                        player must wear robe/medallion and have the lamb
                           corpse (objects/lambbody) to
                           'take bowl from pedestal'
 
SANCT.C     - Central Sanctuary of the Temple; where 'final rite' is done
              quest: This is where the 'final rite' is performed and where
                        the quest EXP is given.
                     Player must wear robe/medallion and have the bowl
                        and lamb corpse to start.
                     'fill bowl with blood' - fills the bowl with blood
                        from the corpse
                     'drink from bowl' - adds VDQDRANK.C invis object to
                        the player
                     'place corpse on altar' - must be dressed and have
                        VDQDRANK.C object
                     'shout praise unto the Light of Darkness and to Our
                        Mother who brings it.' - removes the quest objects
                        and gives the quest exp.
              obj: VDQDRANK.C - invisible object given to player when he
                      does 'drink blood from bowl'; removed at end of quest.
 
STAIR1.C    - Stairwell between levels; first level
              npc: TOWGUARD.C (1) will not allow player to go up unless
                     he has the Captain's seal (objects/cptnseal.c) gotten
                     in CAPTNGRD.C
 
STAIR2.C    - Stairwell between levels; second level
              npc: TOWGUARD.C (1) will not allow player to go up or north
                       unless he has the Captain's seal (objects/cptnseal.c)
                       gotten in CAPTNGRD.C
                   ATKRAVEN.C (1) flies in when player takes her eggs.
                       auto-attacks.
              special: The player can 'look out window' and see a branch
                       with a nest with eggs in it. the player can
                       'take eggs from nest' to get 2 eggs (objects/ravenegg.c)
                       These have nothing to do with anything else (quest),
                       just something to do. When player takes eggs, the
                       mother raven flies in and auto-attacks.
 
STAIR3.C    - Lookout tower of the temple - can "see" Argos, forest, mountains
              npc: TOWGUARD.C (3)
 
TALIS.C     - Chamber of the Keeper of the Talisman
              npc:   TALISPRS.C - gives some quest hints
              quest: Player must get the priestess to bless the sacramental
                        wine found in WINERY.C. This blessing makes the
                        wine poisonous. The poison object is needed to
                        "be in the right state of mind" to pray in CHAPEL.C
                     'ask priestess for blessing' Priestess says to 'pray'
                     'pray for blessing' if player wears robe/medallion and
                        has bottle of sacramental wine, priestess blesses the
                        wine and makes it poisonous.
 
 
TDPATHx.C   - Path from forest to temple
 
WINERY.C    - The winery/brewery of the temple
              quest: The sacramental wine needed is found here
              obj:   VISWINE.C - the sacramental wine.
                        If player is wearing robe/medallion, can
                        'take wine from table' to get it.
