------------------------------------------------------------------------
      Gennisi Ceremony ("Birth" - Seeker joining Elemental Clerics)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preparations:
- The person joining is called the Seeker.
- After joining, the Seeker is then referred to as a Child.
- The person leading the ceremony is called the Primary Member (PM)
- The person sponsoring the Seeker is called the Sponsor (SP)
  (The PM can also be an SP if nobody else is available).
- All participants must be wearing white robes, especially the
  PM, SP and Seeker.
- The SP must be wearing a mantle.
- The PM must be wearing a mantle and alb.
- The PM must carry a quantity of incense (two or three is a good idea).
- The PM must carry a water vial and bread (both found in the Sacristy
  cabinet).
- The PM must carry an anointing oil (found in the Sacristy cabinet).
- All participants should gather in the private chapel where the
  ceremony must take place.
- NOTE: Once the ceremony has begun (the incense is loaded), the chapel
  doors will be locked to all non-members, and will not unlock until
  the incense burns out.
- NOTE: Do not use 'say to <Seeker>' unless you want to have it look
  funny (i.e. 'say to john hi' would look like 'Bob says to John who
  is praying 'hi').

Ceremony:
1  The PM sets the Ceremony (ceremony Gennisi) and then formally 
   welcomes all and announces/introduces the Seeker.
2  The PM casts a pentagram on the floor (cast pentagram).
3  The PM starts the incense with the command 'load incense'. It will
   start to burn immediately, but will take a while to fill the room.
   While you are waiting for this to happen, the PM will have the 
   Seeker stand in the pentagram (stand in pentagram).
4  The PM asks the Seeker to affirm his/her willingness to abide by
   the objectives of the Circle ("I do" type of deal).
5  The PM faces the Seeker to the Northeast (face <Seeker> northeast).
6  The PM says:
   "I call to Lord Diabrecho, Powers and creatures of Water"
   "Greet this Seeker and give him/her your blessings"
7  The PM offers the Seeker his/her chalice to drink from 
   (offer <Seeker>). This will automatically consume the water vial.
8  The PM faces the Seeker to the Southeast (face <Seeker> southeast).
9  The PM says:
   "I call to Lord Pyros, Powers and creatures of Fire".
   "Greet this Seeker and give him/her your blessings".
10 The PM touches the Seeker on his/her right shoulder with his/her
   Fire Wand (rtouch <Seeker>).
11 The PM faces the Seeker to the Southwest (face <Seeker> southwest).
12 The PM says:
   "I call to Lady Gu, Powers and creatures of Earth".
   "Greet this Seeker and give him/her your blessings".
13 The PM feeds the Seeker from his/her pentacle (efeed <Seeker>). This 
   will automatically consume the bread.
14 The PM faces the Seeker to the Northwest (face <Seeker> northwest).
15 The PM says:
   "I call to Lady Aeria, Powers and creatures of Air".
   "Greet this Seeker and give him/her your blessings".
16 The PM touches the Seeker on his/her left shoulder with his/her
   Air Dagger (ltouch <Seeker>).
17 The PM faces the Seeker to the North (face <Seeker> north).
18 The PM says:
   "I call to Psuchae, Powers and creatures of Spirit".
   "Greet this Seeker and give him/her your blessings".
19 The PM touches the Seeker on his/her forehead with his/her
   Sacred Mandala (ftouch <Seeker>).   
20 The PM says:
   "Creators, we bring this Seeker before You".
   "Asking that You embrace him/her in body and spirit".
   "Guide him/her upon his/her path".
   "And witness that we accept him/her into our Family".
21 The PM has the Seeker kneel (kneel).   
22 The PM asks "Who has offered to guide this Seeker?"
23 The SP answers "I do" and lays his/her hands on the 
   Seeker (lay hands on <Member>).
24 The PM does likewise. Any other members present may do 
   likewise now, if they choose or the PM deems it 
   necessary or wanted.
25 The PM anoints the Seeker with oil (anoint <Seeker>).
26 The SP confirms choosing the Seeker (echoose <Seeker>).
27 The PM announces the joining with something like "With your pledge
   of fidelity and the support of those present, I accept you to the
   Circle as a Cleric of the Elementals".
28 The Seeker prays to the Elementals (pray to the Elementals).
29 The PM confirms they have joined and then announces them as
   a Child of the Elementals. (the Child is experiencing a vision
   for a few moments (about 10 seconds), give them time to do so)
30 Ask the Child to rise.
31 The PM reminds the Child that they should seek out Jardeth
   and ask him about their Sporos (and you'll probably want
   to give them a Ceremonial Dagger to take with them).
32 The PM formally ends the Ceremony (ceremony end).

Summary of necessary commands:
'ceremony Gennisi'
'cast pentagram'
'load censor'              (must have incense)
'stand in pentagram'
'offer <Seeker>'           ex: 'offer Jaacar'
'rtouch <Seeker>'          ex: 'rtouch Jaacar'
'efeed <Seeker>'           ex: 'efeed Jaacar'
'ltouch <Seeker>'          ex: 'ltouch Jaacar'
'ftouch <Seeker>'          ex: 'ftouch Jaacar'
'lay hands on <Seeker>'    ex: 'lay hands on Jaacar'
'anoint <Seeker>           ex: 'anoint Jaacar'
'echoose <Seeker>'         ex: 'echoose Jaacar'
'ceremony end'


------------------------------------------------------------------------
     Enarxi Ceremony ("Commencement" - Child becoming Master Cleric)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preparations:
- The person being promoted is called the Child.
- After joining, the Child is then referred to as a Master or Cleric.
- The person leading the ceremony is called the Primary Member (PM)
- All participants must be wearing white robes, especially the Child.
- The PM must be wearing a mantle and alb.
- The PM must carry a quantity of incense (two or three is a good idea).
- The PM must carry an anoining oil (found in the Sacristy cabinet).
- The PM should have a speech ready for the ceremony including any 
  special deeds the Child has taken to get to this point and what it
  means to be a Master prepared beforehand.
- The Child should be prepared to follow the instructions of the PM and
  should have reasons they are thankful to each Diety prepared
  beforehand.
- All participants should gather in the private chapel where the
  ceremony must take place.
- NOTE: Once the ceremony has begun (the incense is loaded), the chapel
  doors will be locked to all non-members, and will not unlock until
  the incense burns out.
- NOTE: Do not use 'say to <Seeker>' unless you want to have it look
  funny (i.e. 'say to john hi' would look like 'Bob says to John who
  is praying 'hi').

Ceremony:
1  The PM sets the Ceremony (ceremony Enarxi) and then formally 
   welcomes all and announces/introduces Child.
2  The PM casts a pentagram on the floor (cast pentagram).
3  The PM starts the incense with the command 'load incense'. It will
   start to burn immediately, but will take a while to fill the room.
   While you are waiting for this to happen, the PM will have the 
   Child stand in the pentagram (stand in pentagram).
4  The PM asks the Child to reaffirm his/her willingness to abide by
   the objectives of the Circle in their new rank ("I do" type of deal).
5  The PM asks the Child to kneel.
6  The PM says:
   "Child, the delusion of anger becomes the mirror like wisdom. Turn 
   to the Northeast and thank Lord Diabrecho for guidance"
   (Child does 'turn northeast').
7  The Child turns to the Northeast and thanks Lord Diabrecho for
   guidance (the Child should give the reasons they are thankful for
   Lord Diabrecho's aid).
8  The Child prays to Lord Diabrecho (pray to Lord Diabrecho).
9  The PM says:
   "Child, the delusion of attachment becomes the wisdom of discernment.
   Turn to the Southeast and thank Lord Pyros for guidance"
   (Child does 'turn southeast').
10 The Child turns to the Southeast and thanks Lord Pyros for
   guidance (the Child should give the reasons they are thankful for
   Lord Pyros' aid).
11 The Child prays to Lord Pyros (pray to Lord Pyros).
12 The PM says:
   "Child, the delusion of jealousy becomes the wisdom of accomplishment.
   Turn to the Southwest and thank Lady Gu for guidance"
   (Child does 'turn southwest').
13 The Child turns to the Southwest and thanks Lady Gu for
   guidance (the Child should give the reasons they are thankful for
   Lady Gu's aid).
14 The Child prays to Lady Gu (pray to Lady Gu).
15 The PM says:
   "Child, the delusion of ignorance becomes the wisdom of reality.
   Turn to the Northwest and thank Lady Aeria for guidance"
   (Child does 'turn northwest').
16 The Child turns to the Northwest and thanks Lady Aeria for
   guidance (the Child should give the reasons they are thankful for
   Lady Aeria's aid).
17 The Child prays to Lady Aeria (pray to Lady Aeria).
18 The PM says:
   "Child, the delusion of pride becomes the wisdom of humility. 
   Turn to the North and thank Psuchae for guidance"
   (Child does 'turn north').
19 The Child turns to the North and thanks Psuchae for
   guidance (the Child should give the reasons they are thankful for
   Psuchae's aid).
20 The Child prays to Psuchae (pray to Psuchae).
21 The PM gives their speech about how much dedication it took for
   the Child to get to this point and what it is to be a Master.   
22 The PM anoints the Member with oil by doing the command 'anoint 
   <Member>'.
23 The PM asks the Child if they are prepared to fulfil their duties
   and responsibilities as a Master ("I will" type of deal).
24 The PM promotes the Member using the 'promote <Member> Master'
   command.
25 The PM asks the Master to rise.
26 The PM instructs the Master to seek out the Conduit Chamber and 
   once there they should <seek> the visions.
27 The PM erases the pentagram (erase pentagram). 
28 The PM formally ends the Ceremony (ceremony end).

Summary of necessary commands:
'ceremony Enarxi'
'cast pentagram'

'stand in pentagram'
'turn <direction>'         ex: 'turn North'
'pray to <diety>'          ex: 'pray to Lord Pyros'
'anoint <Member>           ex: 'anoint Jaacar'

'erase pentagram'
'ceremony end'

- All participants should be wearing white robes, especially the Member.
- The PM may wear a mantle and alb.
- The PM must carry an anointing oil (found in the Sacristy cabinet).
- The new ranking to be given to the Member during the ceremony
  should be determined AHEAD of time.
- The PM must carry a quantity of incense (two or three is a good idea).
- All participants should gather in the chapel where the ceremony
  must take place.
- NOTE: Once the ceremony has begun (the incense is loaded), the chapel
  doors will be locked to all non-members, and will not unlock until
  the incense burns out.
- NOTE: Do not use 'say to <Seeker>' unless you want to have it look
  funny (i.e. 'say to john hi' would look like 'Bob says to John who
  is praying 'hi').

   welcomes all and announces/introduces Member.
2  The PM casts a pentagram on the floor (cast pentagram).

   start to burn immediately, but will take a while to fill the room.
   While you are waiting for this to happen, the PM will have the 
   Member stand in the pentagram (stand in pentagram).
4  The PM asks the Member to reaffirm his/her willingness to abide by
   the objectives of the Circle in their new rank ("I do" type of deal)
5  The PM asks the Member to kneel.
5  The PM anoints the Member with oil by doing the command 'anoint 
   <Member>'.
