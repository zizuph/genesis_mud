found in ~zima/argos/metro/monsters
 
alexis.c    - Quest master.
              ROOM: park5.c (roams parkX.c via add_act(north/south/east/west)
              ASK:  'for tour' - gives player a tour and sets quest prop
                                 accordingly (indicating on tour)
                    'for reward' - gives player reward if tour finished;
                                 sets quest bits appropriately; sets
                                 quest prop (indicating not on tour)
barpatron.c - A barpatron - a randomized, drunk NPC who stays in the
              pub.
              ROOM: pub.c (2)
bartender.c - Bartender in the pub. If he is not present, no pub functions
              work
              ROOM: pub.c
brassman.c  - Keeper of the Brass Works Shop. Player can't buy anything
              if this NPC not present.
              ROOM: brasswrk.c
child.c     - A randomized child.
              ROOM: park2.c (2), park4.c (2), park6.c(2)
citizen.c   - A randomized citizen.
              Evil citizens are rude to visitors (rude when player intros)
              while good align citizens are nice (return intro when
              player intros).
              ROOM: proad9.c (2), proad13.c (2)
                    droad9.c (2), droad13.c (2)
                    ttroad9.c(2), trroad13.c(2)
                    troad13.c(2)
curator.c  - Curator of the Museum in the library.
             He is part of the Stolen Cassock quest. He will answer some
             questions about the stolen item and the thieves. When a
             player on the quest returns the stolen cassock to him,
             he sets the ARGOS_QUEST_PROP to quest done.
             ROOM: lib3.c
glass_blower.c - Keeper of the Glass Works Shop. Shop functions won't work
             if he is not present.
             ROOM: glasswrk.c
hkrcommander.c - Hidden Kretan Commander
             Leader of a group of kretans hiding out in the empty shop.
             ROOM: empty.c
hkretan.c   - Hidden Kretan Warrior
             ROOM: empty.c (3)
innkeeper.c - The keeper of the inn. Inn functions (buy/rent) won't work
             if he is not in the inn.
             ROOM: inn.c
merchant.c  - The keeper of the shop. Shop functions won't work if he is not
             in the shop.
             ROOM: shop.c
pigeon_keeper.c - The keeper of the coop. Can't buy pigeons if he is not in
             the coop.
             ROOM: coop.c
seamstress.c - Seamstress of the Woolworks. She provides the services of
             spinning and weaving. Services won't work if she is not there.
             ROOM: woolwrk.c
smith.c     - The smith of the forge. Forge functions won't work if he is not
             in the forge (wax/sharpen)
             ROOM: forge.c
speaker.c   - He speaks at the Aeropagus (public forum). He is randomly
              good (pro-visitor/king) or evil (anti-visitor). He just
              shouts out his speeches.
              ROOM: aeropagus.c
strguard.c  - Street Guard of the City. Does not wander.
              ROOM: lroad7.c (2), lroad10.c (2), lroad13.c (2), lroad16.c(2)
                    proad11.c(2), droad11.c (2), trroad11.c(2), troad11.c(2)
                    aroad7.c (2), aroad11.c (2), aroad16.c(2)
thesp_app  - Apprentice of the Thespian guild.
             ROOM: amph2.c, amph3.c amph4.c (1 each)
thespian.c - Member of the Thespian Guild
             ROOM: thesp1.c (3), thesp2.c (2)
thespmstr.c - Master of the Thespian Guild.
             He is involved with the New Wine quest. When a player brings
             him a bottle of new wine, the quest is done.
             ROOM: thesp2.c
vcnoble.c  - Hostess at the visitors center. She will answer a few questions
             about the Registry Tour (about quill, bursar, etc.)
             ROOM: visitctr.c
 
ZIMA/ARGOS/PHARTEMP/MONSTERS
outpriest.c - Priest who roams around outside the closed temple. if a
              player asks him 'to bless grape juice', and the player
              has a bottle of grape juice, he will turn the juice
              into wine. Part of New Wine Quest.
