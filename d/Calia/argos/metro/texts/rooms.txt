******************************************************************************
*                            EXPLANATION OF ROOMS                            *
******************************************************************************
Many rooms are "simple" which means they have no special commands and are not
involved in a quest (ie Road rooms). No description of simple rooms is given
here. See map.txt for a map of these rooms.
All objects  (OBJ:) are found in ~zima/argos/<area>/objects
All monsters (NPC:) are found in ~zima/argos/<area>/monsters
All weapons  (WEP:) are found in ~zima/argos/<area>/weapons
All armours  (ARM:) are found in ~zima/argos/<area>/armours
 
ZIMA/ARGOS/METRO/ROOMS/*
------------------------
aeropagus.c - The Aeropagus is the outdoor open forum of the city. A speaker
              is cloned here making a speech, as well as 2 citizens. The
              statue of Argosius (part of Toe Quest) is found here.
              NPC: speaker.c, citizen.c (2)
              CMD: 'rub toe of statue' (Toe Quest)
 
amph1.c     - Entrance to the Amphitheatre. Statue of Thema here for Toe
              Quest.
              CMD: 'rub toe of statue'
amph2&3.c   - Amphitheatre seating area. Apprentices of Thespian Guild
              cleaning up here.
              NPC: thesp_app.c (1 each)
amph4.c     - Amphitheatre stage. Accessible only from Thespian guild room,
              not from seating area. One apprentice here
              NPC: thesp_app.c
arch.c      - Statue of Cosmopolitus here, part of toe quest.
              CMD: 'rub toe of statue'
aroadXY.c   - Alpharon Road (east/west along south side);
              NPC: strguard.c (2 each at aroad7.c, aroad11.c, aroad16.c)
bank.c      - Standard banking functions. Also part of Registry Quest.
              Player can 'ask bursar for quill'. If the player is on
              the quest, the Bursar will give him a quill. (Bursar is
              "behind window" and therefore not a real NPC, only an illusion
              of the descriptions of the room.)
              CMD: 'ask bursar for quill'
              OBJ: reg_quill.c cloned when player on tour and does command
boardrm.c   - City Boardroom (part of the Visitor's Center)
              OBJ: ~zima/argos/metro/rooms/boardrm/board.c
brasswrk.c  - Brass Works Shop. Can buy a lamp or a pair of sheep shears
              here.
              CMD: 'buy lamp', 'buy shears'
              NPC: brassman.c
              OBJ: brass_lamp.c, sheep_shears.c (when purchased)
coop.c      - Pigeon Shop. Player can buy messenger pigeon here.
              CMD: 'buy pigeon' (won't work if keeper not here)
              NPC: pigeon_keeper.c
              OBJ: /d/Genesis/obj/pigeon.c (std Genesis pigeon; when bought)
depot.c     - Depot (called the Strathmos) where transports will leave/arrive
              to Calia docks.
droad11.c   - Statue of Basileus found here for toe quest.
              CMD: 'rub toe of statue'
              NPC: strguard.c (2)
droad9&13.c - NPC: citizen.h (2 each)
empty.c     - "Empty/unused" shop of commercial district. Kretans have moved
              in to make it a secret hiding place. A player must bust through
              the door to get in (see proad9.c)
              NPC: hkrcommander.c (1), hkretan.c (3)
forge.c     - City Forge. Players can get weapons waxed and sharpened here.
              (Coding for fixing weapons left in, but not available to
              players)
              CMD: 'wax <weapon>', 'sharpen <weapon>', 'mend <weapon>'
                   (no service given if smith not here)
              NPC: smith.c
glasswrk.c  - Glass Works Shop. Glass products can be bought here, including
              an empty bottle (needed to get grape juice from press in press.c)
              and an empty vial (needed to get olive oil from press).
              CMD: 'buy bottle','buy vial'(won't work if glass blower not here)
              NPC: glass_blower.c
              OBJ: glass_vial.c, glass_bottle.c (when bought)
inn.c       - Standard Inn - can buy food. Can also 'rent room' which allows
              player to used inn2.c as a temp start loc.
              CMD: 'rent room'->sets prop in player so can do 'start here'
                     in inn2.c
                   Standard 'buy food' commands
                   (no commands work if inn keeper not here)
              NPC: inn_keeper.c
inn2.c      - Bedroom of Inn. Can be "rented over night" as a temp start loc.
              Can get instructions by reading card in the room.
              CMD: 'start here' - can do if 'rent room' in inn.c
lib2/4.c    - Library; can read books on History of Argos
              CMD: 'read <selection>'
              note: texts of books read are in zima/argos/metro/texts/lib
lib3.c      - Museum of collection of Alexis travels. Part of Stolen Cassock
              Quest. Can ask curator 'about theft' and 'about thief' to
              get info needed to find and return the Cassock here. All
              commands done in NPC.
              NPC: curator.c
libhall2.c  - West Hallway of Library - Statue of Biblionus here for Toe Quest
              CMD: 'rub toe on statue'
lroadXY.c   - Lambdos Road (east-west along north side)
              NPC: strguard.c (2 each at lroad7,10,11,16.c)
park2/4/6.c - City Park
              NPC: child.c (2 each)
park5.c     - City Park. Where Alexis (Quest Master) is cloned
              NPC: alexis.c
post.c      - Standard Post Office
press.c     - Royal Press of Argos. There is a press here which can press
              grapes into grape juice and olives into olive oil. Grapes
              are found/picked in ~zima/argos/nwterr/rooms/vyard*.c; olives
              are found/picked in ~zima/argos/swterr/rooms/ogrove*.c.
              The player needs an empty bottle (objects/glass_bottle.c) to
              get grape juice and an empty vial (objects/glass_vial.c) to
              get olive oil. (If containers not brought, juice/oil runs
              out on floor). Bottle/vial purchased in rooms/glasswrk.c
              CMD: 'place grapes in bin','pull lever'
                   if player has empty bottle, it is replaced with a bottle
                   of grape juice (objects/grape_juice)
              CMD: 'place olives in bin','pull lever'
                   if player has empty vial, a function is called in the
                   vial to "fill" it (objects/glass_vial.c)
              OBJ: grape_juice.c, glass_vial.c (obtained as described)
                   press.c (non-movable furniture)
proadXY.c   - Protos Road (north-south on west side of town).
              NPC: citizen.c  (2 each in proad9&13.c)
              NPC: strguard.c (2 in proad11.c)
pub.c       - Standard Pub with bartender and 2 barpatrons.
              CMD: standard pub functions (won't work if bartender not here)
              NPC: bartender.c, barpatron.c (2)
sculptor.c  - Sculptor's Studio. Incomplete. In future will have sculptor/
              painter who can make sculptures and paint pictures. Will
              be part of a future quest. Now, just a freebie knife is
              found there.
              WEP: czdagger.c (1 per reset if not still there)
shop.c      - Standard Shop.
              CMD: standard shop commands (won't work if merchant not here)
              NPC: merchant.c
shoprm.c    - Inventory of shop; inaccessible by players.
thesp1.c    - Front room of Thespian guild
              NPC: thespian.c (3)
thesp2.c    - Back room of Thespian guild
              NPC: thespian.c (2)
                   thespmstr.c - part of New Wine Quest. When a player on th
                                 quest gives this NPC a bottle of new wine,
                                 the player has finished the quest.
toequest.c  - NOT A ROOM - includes functions needed for the Toe Quest.
              All rooms involved in the Toe Quest include this file.
              (contains code for 'rub toe on statue' command)
troad9.c    - Tetartos Road (north-south along east side of town)
              Empty shop is to the west. No exit available
              when updated/reset. Player must 'kick boards' repeatedly
              until the boards break, at which time the 'west' exit to
              the "empty" shop is opened (see rooms/empty.c)
troad11.c   - Tetartos Road
              NPC: strguard.c (2)
troad13.c   - Tetartos Road
              NPC: citizen.h (2)
trroad9.c   - Tritos Road (north-south through middle of town)
              Statue of Agrarius here for Toe Quest.
              CMD: 'rub toe on statue' (toe quest cmd)
              NPC: citizen.c (2)
trroad11.c  - Tritos Road
              NPC: strguard.c (2)
trroad13.c  - Tritos Road
              NPC: citizen.h(2)
visitctr.c  - Visitor's Center.
              Part of Registry Quest. A registry is found on a pedestal here.
              The player may 'sign registry' if he has a quill
              (objects/reg_quill) and does 'dip quill in ink'. This solves
              the quest. The hostess npc may be asked questions about the
              quill, bursar, etc. Free food is also available.
              CMD: 'dip quill in ink' - must have quill; set's prop in quill
                                        that inidicates it has ink on it
                   'sign registry' - to finish quest. Name and title of
                                     player recorded in book.
                   'read registry' - tail() the registry file
                                     file=~zima/argos/metro/texts/registry
                                     contains name/title of each person who
                                     has signed the registry.
                   'take bachlava from tray' - get free food
                   'take ouzo from tray'     - get free drink
              NPC: vcnoble.c
              OBJ: ouzo.c (4 per reset), bachlava.c (6 per reset)
                   pedestal.c (non-movable furniture)
woolwrk.*  - Wool Works Shop.
             Seamstress here will spin wool into yarn and weave yarn into
             a new tunic or new cloak (a fee for each service). Part of
             Grandma Quest- player must take new cloak/tunic to grandama
             (Giagia) in ~zima/nwterr/rooms/vyardd.c. Need a bale of wool
             and fee to get a spool of yarn spun. Need a spool of yarn
             (each) to get a tunic or cloak made. Wool gotten by shearing
             sheep in ~zima/nwterr/rooms/past*.c. Need shears to shear
             sheep (objects/sheep_shears.c) which are bought in brasswrk.c
             CMD: 'ask seamstress to spin wool into yarn'
                  - take bale of wool and fee from player
                  - after a performance, gives yarn (objects/yarn) to player
                  'ask seamstress to weave wool into a cloak/tunic'
                  - take yarn and fee from player
                  - after a performance, gives cloak/tunic to player
            OBJ: yarn.c, new_tunic.c, new_cloak.c
                 wool_bale.c obtained by use of sheep_shears.c
