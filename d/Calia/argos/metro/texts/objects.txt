ZIMA/ARGOS/METRO/OBJECTS
bachlava.c    - Just some free food. 6 pieces cloned at each reset (if gone)
                ROOM: visitctr.c (6 max per reset)
brass_lamp.c  - A torch which can be purchased in the brass shop. The lamp
                needs oil to run. Oil is obtained by picking olives in
                ~zima/argos/swterr/rooms/ogrove*.c, buying an empty vial
                in rooms/glasswrk.c, and pressing the olives into vials
                of oil.
                The vial of oil may be poured into the lamp to fuel it.
                ROOM: brasswrk.c (purchased here)
doll.c        - A child's toy
                NPC: child.c
glass_bottle.c- An empty glass bottle. Needed when pressing grapes into
                grape juice in rooms/press.c
                ROOM: glasswrk.c (purchased here)
glass_vial.c  - An empty glass vial.   Needed when pressing olives into
                olive oil in rooms/press.c. Also needed to fuel the
                brass lamp (when full).
                ROOM: glasswrk.c (purchased here), press.c (filled here)
grape_juice.c - A bottle of grape juice exchanged for an empty bottle
                when a player presses grapes.
                ROOM: press.c (obtained by pressing grapes & having bottle)
ouzo.c        - Free Drink gotten in Visitor Center (max 4 per reset)
                ROOM: visitctr.c
press.c       - Non-movable furniture object
                ROOM: press.c
regquill.c    - Quill used in the Registry Tour. Obtained from Bursar in
                bank, used to sign registry in VC.
                ROOM: bank.c (via 'ask bursar for quill')
                      must be on quest to get it
sheep_shears.c- Used to shear sheep to get their wool.
                When wielded, player can 'shear sheep with shears'.
                If any object id("sheep") in room, player gets
                a bale of wool (objects/wool_bale) and a property is
                added to the "sheep" to indicate it has been sheared.
                Can not shear same sheep twice. Wool obtained needed
                to make yarn in wool works for Grandma Quest.
                ROOM: brasswrk.c (bought here)
toy*.c        - Random children's toys
                NPC: child.c
vcpedestal.c  - Non-movable furniture
                ROOM: visitctr.c
wool_bale.c   - A bale of wool obtained from the shears when shearing
                a sheep.
                OBJ: obtained by use of sheep_shears.c object
yarn.c        - A spool of yarn spun by the seamstress in the woolworks
                Needed for her to weave tunic/cloak for Grandma quest
                ROOM: woolwrk.c (obtained via 'ask seamstress to spin...')
 
ZIMA/ARGOS/PHARTEMP/OBJECTS
new_wine.c    - obtained when Pharosian Priest blesses a bottle of grape
                juice. Part of New Wine Quest
                NPC: outpriest.c
