
For an estimate of the repair cost for weapons:
    wcost <item>

To wax a corroded weapon:
    wax <item>

To sharpen a dull weapon:
    sharpen <item>

I cannot mend broken weapons.

For an estimate of the repair cost for armour:
    acost <item> 

To fix an item of armour (but I cannot mend broken armours):
    fix <item>
