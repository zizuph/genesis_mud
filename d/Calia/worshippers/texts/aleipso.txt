

    The Elemental Power of Aleipso
    ==============================

    The special gift patrons of Lord Diabrecho receive is the
    power to heal wounds.  This power requires a good skill 
    in the art of water spells and a moderate skill in the
    art of enchantment spells, as well as some skill in 
    spellcraft.  You can use this power to heal yourself, 
    other members of the circle, or those  you feel worthy 
    of healing outside of the circle. You must have cladina 
    to use this gift.
