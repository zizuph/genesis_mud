

    The Elemental Power of Aelikias
    ===============================

    One of the gifts you recieve from Lord Pyros is the power
    to check a persons inner flame to divine their age.  This 
    power requires a small skill in the art of fire spells and 
    a small skill in the art of divination spells, as well as 
    some skill in spellcraft.  You must have chicory to use 
    this gift.
