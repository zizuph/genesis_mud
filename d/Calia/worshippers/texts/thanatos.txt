

    The Elemental Power of Thanatos
    ===============================

    One of the gifts you recieve from Lady Gu is the power to 
    divine who the killer of another is by praying at their
    corpse.  This power requires a small skill in both the arts
    of divination and earth spells, as well as some skill in 
    spellcraft.  You must have a rock to use this gift.
