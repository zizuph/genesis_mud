


    Lord Pyros, Elemental of Fire, Lord of Flame
    ============================================

    Lord Pyros is a muscular warrior-prince with eyes like flames
    and flowing red locks of hair and beard.  He wields a great
    flaming sword and bellows with a voice like a roaring furnace.
    Unforgiving and unswayable, showing disrespect to him invites
    immediate immolation.  He is the most intense and dangerous
    of the Elementals.
