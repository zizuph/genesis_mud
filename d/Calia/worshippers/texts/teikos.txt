

    The Elemental Power of Teikos
    =============================

    One of the gifts you recieve from Lady Gu is the power to 
    protect yourself from harm with a barrier of light.  This
    power requires a good skill in both the arts of divination
    and earth spells, as well as some skill in spellcraft.  The
    effects of this power will stay with you for a short period
    of time.  You must have a rock to use this gift.
