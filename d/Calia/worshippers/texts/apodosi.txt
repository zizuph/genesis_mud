

    The Elemental Power of Apodosi
    ==============================

    This power, granted by Lady Gu, will transport you back to
    the Temple from any location in the Realms. You need skill
    in conjuration and earth magic in order to use it. Keep in
    mind that the spell is somewhat slow to cast, and will not
    help you escape from a fight. To use it you need a gem:
    an iridescent opal.
