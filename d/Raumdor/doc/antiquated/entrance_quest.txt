KHIRAA ENTRANCE QUEST
=====================

This quest is devided up into parts.

PART ONE -

Quest NPC
     - Gracia - Witch
     - Gives out Quest and explains what needs to be done to obtain
     - first piece.
     - Must obtain 5 pieces of the pentagram. 
     - Each piece is a portion of a demigod that was fractured
     - when the gateway was sealed.

PART TWO -
     - Obtain piece of pentagram. 
     - Each piece will give exp.
     - Each piece stays with player until quest is completed.
     - Each gained piece will lead you to the next piece.
        

PART THREE -
     - Return to Gracia - Witch with all five pieces.
     - She will cast a spell to bind the pieces together although
     - they are fracturd. She tells player to find the temple.
     - The temple has random location within the forest.
     - She rewards player with experience and now pieces are grouped

PART FOUR -
     - The temple
     - There are 25 rooms within the temple. 
     - Each room holds a pentagram and a coven of witches.
     - Player must kill the witches in the room to be able to use the
     - newly formed pieces to invert the pentagram.
     - When all 25 rooms pentagram are inverted the witch Gracia appears.
     - She is pleased that now she may enter HER TEMPLE as the coven 
     - of witches cast her out with their foul magicks.
     - She rewards the player with experience giving them passage to hell
     - So the may now forge the pentagram together.
     - Player decends into hell, a fiery furnace is there for the player 
     - to forge the pentagram together, upon completion they are trans to
     - a random open area gaining exp.

PART FIVE -
     - The newly forged pentagram cries out to the player to feed 
     - it the blood of the innocent. It will cry out giving hints until
     - it is fed the blood.
     - The player must find a pure white unicorn.
     - Then sacrifice it upon a specially laid altar where there is a 
     - groove that the player puts the pentagram under and it fills with 
     - the innocents blood casuing it to invert.
     - The player gains experience!!
     - Also now may see the hidden entrance to the boat.

PART SIX -
     - The boat travels the river of dead.
     - Player and arrives at the Khiraa Temple
     - Player receives exp.
