Name:           screech
Object:         /d/Terel/minstrels/dark/Power/blast
Cost:           23 mana, Components: instrument, songbook.
Casting Time:   5
Skills:         SS_DEX, SS_DIS, SG_READ_MUSIC, SG_VOCAL, SG_INSTRUMENTAL
Resolution:     Result = resolve_task(TASK_SIMPLE, ((SS_DEX+SG_INSTRUMENTAL)+
			 (SS_DIS+SG_VOCAL)+Guild_Stat*2))
Failure:        Result = -1   the song fails completely;
			  0   the song couldn't be played;
			 1-20 the song succeeds somewhat but with possible 
			      accompanying instrument damage.
			 >20  the song succeeds completely.

Description:    Causes a temporary reduction in opponents dexterity for
                20 seconds.  Maximum possible rate is 75% reduction. 
                Reduction is always expressed in a percentage, to be
                fair.
		Amount of damage = (20 + Result) * Effectiveness modifier.
		Effectiveness modifier is a function of the condition of
		the instrument (or vocal chords) and whether or not the
		performer is part of a choir.  It is expressed as a percent.
		Amount of reduction is reduced if living is magic resistant by
		Amount of reduction is never greater than 75%.
Duration:       temporary (20 seconds before original value restores)
Requirements:   SG_READ_MUSIC > 20
