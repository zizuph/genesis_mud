(<name> of the <family name> the <title>, <mortal level>, male/female haradrim)

Finwe ahn toh Moshe'Kaan toh Dai Menfaz, champion, male haradrim
Toby ahn toh Opode'Nam toh Xae Kaola, champion, male haradrim
Lucia ahn toh Opode'Nam toh Konshao, hero, female haradrim
