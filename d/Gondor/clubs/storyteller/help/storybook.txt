                     ---=== Storyteller's Club ===---


        The following actions are valid for use with your storybook:

        - copy        - copy a story from the archives to your storybook
        - erase       - erase a story from your storybook
        - measure     - measure the length of a story in your storybook
        - read        - read the list of stories in your storybook, or a
			particular story
        - spin        - spin a delightful tale from your storybook
        - stop        - stop spinning a tale
