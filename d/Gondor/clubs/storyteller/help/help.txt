                     ---=== Storyteller's Club ===---


        The following emotes are currently available for Storytellers.

        - stadjust    - Adjust your spectacles before continuing.
        - stapplaud*  - Applaud someone's storytelling of <type>
        - stbid       - Bid someone farewell.
        - stbook*     - Flip through your book for a story of <type>
        - stclean     - Clean your spectacles.
        - stclear     - Clear your throat to begin telling a story.
        - stedge      - Sit on the edge of your seat listening to the story.
        - stemphasis  - Shake your spectacles to emphasize something.
        - stgaze      - Gaze thoughtfully at your storybook.
        - stgreet     - Greet someone.
        - stidea      - You have an idea for a story.
        - stlisten    - Listen to the story being told.
        - stlost      - Stare into space lost in thoughts.
        - stobserve   - Observe everything around you.
        - stpause     - Pause your telling for a moment.
        - stpeer      - Peer at someone over your spectacles.
        - strub       - Rub your fingertips.
        - stsigh      - Sigh wistfully while reminiscing.
        - stsmile     - Smile at someone.
        - ststory     - Study everybody to consider if there's a story to spin.
        - sttear      - Wipe a tear from your eye.

	Emotes with a * can use the following types:
	    joy, sorrow, comedy, romance, horror, suspense,

	Help also available with 'help teller storybook'.
