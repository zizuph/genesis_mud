08-g-leaves : game: The last leaves of Fall.

Distribute one colored (5 different ones) gondorian oak leaf to each person
in the game. Also in Gondor some will be hidden.

As a delicate leaf floats down into your hands, a tiny voice whispers 
in your head: Gather one of each colour in the space of an hour and 
much luck will be yours!

25K general experience for every winner after an hour.
