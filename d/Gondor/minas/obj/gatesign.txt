          WELCOME TO MINAS TIRITH, CAPITAL OF GONDOR!
            --------------------------------------
       Here you can find -
            - a place to stay overnight.
            - good food and drinks.
            - pawnshops and equipment makers.
            - post-office, bank, training facilities.
            - transport to other cities of the world.
            -----------------------------------------
       Roads here lead:
       North: Anorien, and on to Rohan.

       North-east: Osgiliath. CAUTION! Going to
         Osgiliath is not advised, if you be not well
         prepared, for that is a territory of war!

       South: Harlond, the harbour of Minas Tirith, and
         on to Lebennin and the city of Pelargir.
