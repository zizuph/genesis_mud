===============================================================================

palanquendo  Far tell.                               
             ingredient: copper coin

mortirio     Grant someone (or self) vision in darkness. 
             ing: black potato (herb)

naro         Command others to do your bidding.          
             three forms of the spell...
             drego nin! - flee me..  target flees.
             libo makil! - break weapon
             maetho - attack other <someone> maetho <someone>
             ing: skull, knife or sumac (the more ingredients
             the better the effect, in some cases might be cast
             without any ingredient, if high guildstat

arhalgul     Attempt to reveal the secrets of an object.    
             simple indetif. spell
             ing: arhal potion quaffed

dramalgos    Stormwind.
             windbolt
             ing: part of a corpse and oregano or basil or
             tarragon or chervil

faugoroth    Choking horror.  
             A stun spell, target stops fighting
             ing: mint

sauthacuith  Drain the lifeforce of a living.               
             drain spell, plus it grants some con
             ing: nenamarth potion and attanar
             notes: Rarely used.

ringurth     Conjure a protective shroud of death.           
             grants extra ac, magic aura
             ing: berichelk potion and fennel or ginger

narusse      Summon the fires of Orodruin upon a blade. 
             flame blade, grants some extra flame damage at
             every hit, plus increases wielder weapon skill
             ing: red pepper plus bragollach potion

dollost      Emptyhead.                                    
             mana drains from victim, portion is given to caster
             ing: dollost potion and garlic

ephessul     Summon the protection of Windfence for someone.
             depending on the spell result it grants protection
             from poison, fire, cold and magic (that is a complete
             immunity to so called statcloud)
             ing: foxglove

thunaur      Flames of Destruction.    
             flamebreath
             ing: nennaur potion (lasts about 10 minutes)
                  when a potion is quaffed then with each flamebreath
                  you will use one part of a corpse

nifarist     Make someone forget who you are.
             as easy as it says :)
             ing: platinum coin, handasse

gostangwedh  Chains of Terror.
             raises encumberance of the targer
             ing: an item must be designated as an ingredient plus 
             some herb

artirith     Enter the realm of shadows.
             invis
             ing: lominen potion

crabandir    Summon a craban to spy on someone.
             craban
             ing: corcosunk potion and holly berry

morthul      Black Breath.  
             ing: morgurth potion and morgurth herb

sulambar     Summon the Wind of Doom.                     
             statcloud
             ing: nenamarth potion and hemlock

yalarochrama Summon a winged steed. 
             beast
             ing: corcosunk potion and any gem
