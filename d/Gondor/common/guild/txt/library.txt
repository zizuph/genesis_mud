
Topic: library

Commands:

- list
    List the titles that have been submitted for inclusion into the library.

- read <title>
    Read one of the submitted titles.

- reject <title>
    Reject one of the submitted titles.
    The book will be destroyed and cannot be recovered.

- add <title> to shelf <shelf>
    Add a submitted title to the library.
    A shelf has to be specified, the shelf has to exist in the library.
