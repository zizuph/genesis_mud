
Topic: impeachment

Commands:

- start/support impeachment of <name>
    Call for the impeachment of a Captain of the Rangers.

- veto the impeachment of <name>
    (Captains only! Only if <name> is not the own name.)
    Cancel the call for the impeachment of a Captain of the Rangers.

- impeach <name>
    Impeach a Captain of the Rangers.
    Only possible if three or more council members support the call
    for the impeachment.
    The Captain and the Lieutenant of the Captain's company will lose
    their offices. An election will be called, with the impeached
    Captain as first candidate. The council will have to 'nominate'
    at least one further candidate.
