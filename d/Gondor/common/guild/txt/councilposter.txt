
                  The Rangers of Gondor Councilroom
                   - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    help council  . . . . .  - Get help on command available to
                               council members.

    vote <text> . . . . . .  - Vote or comment on something under 
                               discussion. The votes can only be 
                               read by the guildmasters.

    call for vote <text>  .  - Call the Council to vote on a topic.
    read call for vote  . .  - Read all present topics that you are
                               asked to vote on.
    read vote <n> . . . . .  - Read the votes on topic number <n>.
    clear call for vote <n>  - Abort voting on topic number <n>.
    terminate vote <n>  . .  - End the voting on topic number <n> and
                               log the results in the library.
    vote on <n> with <vote>  - Vote on topic number <n>. 
                               Accepted votes are yes, aye, no, nay.

    read status  . . . . .  - Read the membership log.

    read punishlist . . . .  - Read the list of possible punishments
    read punishlog  . . . .  - Read the log of the latest punishments
    read pending punishments - Read punishments not yet executed.

    council . . . . . . . .  - See the names of the current Captains 
                               and Lieutenants of the Companies.

    call council  . . . . .  - Call for a meeting with all the 
                               Captains, Lieutenants, and the Chieftain.

    resign  . . . . . . . .  - Resign as a Captain of the Rangers.
    nominate  . . . . . . .  - Nominate a Ranger as candidate for the
     (can only be given when   election of a captain.
     there is no Captain of    If no election has been called yet, this
     the company)              will initiate the election.

    start/support impeachment of <name>
                             - Call for the impeachment of a Captain.
    veto the impeachment of <name>
                             - Stop the call for the impeachment of a
                               Captain (Captains only!)
    impeach (name)           - Impeach a Captain of the Rangers.

    company <name> <company> - Assign a ranger to your company.
     (can be given everywhere)
    deputy <name>            - Assign a ranger of your company to be 
     (can be given everywhere) your deputy. (Captains only!)

    get_key council        - Get a new key to the council chambers
                             if you have lost your old one.
     (only in the rooms outside the council chambers)

    assign <name> specialty <specialty>
                             - Assign a Ranger to working on a specialty.
    assign <name> master     - Assign a Ranger to be Master in their
                               specialty.
    unassign <name>          - Unassigns a Master from their Master
                               position, or a non-Master Ranger from their
                               specialty assignment.
    rmentor <name>           - Assign a Ranger to be a Mentor, giving them
                               the ability to vouch for a pupil.

    pardon <name>            - Pardon a banned Ranger.

    outlaw <name>            - Make a person an Outlaw of the Rangers.
    rpardon <name>           - Pardon an Outlaw of the Rangers.
