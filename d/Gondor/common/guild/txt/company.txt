                           
Topic: company

Commands:

- company <name> <company> 
    Assign a ranger to your company. 
    The command can be given anywhere, but the ranger must be logged in.
    The ranger must be a pupil of the Rangers.

- deputy <name>
    Assign a ranger of your company to become your deputy.
    (Captains only.)
    Can be given anywhere, anytime.
