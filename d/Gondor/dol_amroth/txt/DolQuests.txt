-- * TASKS * --

Discharge ships: (more like a job)

Bring cargo from the ships docked in trade district and `deliver` them in 
warehouse p-09.

-- * QUESTS * --

Tormir charm:

Tormir is somewhere in common district, ask him for help. Buy a rope and
go to hillside/m-11/balchony, tie rope to railing, climb down, search shade
for shovel, dig here. You will find a lucky charm. To get out of the garden
do climb tree, climb to roof, examine what chimney are safe to climb, and
climb it. Find Tormir and give him the charm.     -- 300 xp

Magicians assistent:

The magician needs certain components to research a new spell, you need to
find them for him. It is no rules for where the components are found, only
what it is. Component array will change from time to time.

Assasinate nobel:

One npc hires you to assasin a nobel. What you should do is find this nobel
in his home and kill him. Cut an eye from the corpse as a proof you have killed
him and give it to the contractor.

-- * IDEAS * --

Jewelers account:

The jeweler asks you to get the last account from his shop. The accountant 
is not finished with his numbers, so you have to help him get some papires
before he can give you the account to return to the jeweler.

Wine is a mocker....:

Ask the winemaker to let you down in his cellar and taste his wines with him.
After you have tasted all the sorts he have to offer you will be rewarded.
(mark that you will get drunk, and if you get to drunk to taste the wines
you will be thrown out of the house)

Identify ingridient:

Need alchemy skill and herbalism. The alchemist have lost the lable of some of 
his flasks, you sould sniff, taste and examine them to recogniise what 
ingridients they contain.

Clean beds:

The beds in the barracks is untidy, tidy them. Quest master in harbour office.

Uncover the plot:

A spy works from trade/houses/s-12 and your job is to gather proof against him
and deliver it to the quest master in the harbour office.
