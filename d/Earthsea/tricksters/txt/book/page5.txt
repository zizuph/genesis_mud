       Tricksters guild structure

Tricksters guildstructure is totaly hierarchial.
The Trickster who killed more people gains more 
respect from his companions, and his position in 
the society raises. The most respected Tricksters 
gain the titles of Jamadars, in other words Captains,
and the most legendary and respected Trickster is 
called Jamadar of Jamadars. He holds the rule over
the guild, and is the highest authority for he is
granted the privilage to speak with Kina herself, 
and through his mind the Goddess projects Her will
to Her followers. Tricksters must follow the orders 
of Jamadar of Jamadars, as his will is the will of 
Kina. All Jamadars must be treated with respect, and
the lack of it will be punished. A Trickster advances
