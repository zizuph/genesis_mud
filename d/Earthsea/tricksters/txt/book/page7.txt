      Kina

Kina is dark Goddess of Mischief and Lies. She
enjoys fooling people around, yet her games and
jokes tend to be deadly. The act she enjoys the 
most is always fooling one of her own followers. 
Kina first appeared a long time ago in another 
material plane, when Jolykyar, the Lord of Light,
decided to invoke an ally to aid him in his eternal 
fight against Sikharat, the Lord of Darkness.
Kina was supposed to be a neutral Goddess who would 
bring ballance to the world, and thus neither Light
nor Darkness would come victorious. Yet with time she
gained more and more strength, and soon started to
