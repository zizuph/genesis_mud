     Tricksters history

Tricksters are a very secretive society, better 
known to the common man as the Stranglers. They do 
worship the goddess of Mischief and Lies, Kina. The 
society was founded a long time ago in the lands of 
a distant realm, called Taglios. But with arrival of 
the Black Company they turned to be their most wanted
enemies. After years of wars between the two factions 
the Company turned victorious, and the Masters of 
Shadows were in grave danger. Only Narayan Singh did
survive the conflict, saved by Kina herself who sent
him through dimentions towards our realms. Narayan
