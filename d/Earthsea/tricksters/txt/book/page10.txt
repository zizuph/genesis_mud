       Applying to Tricksters

To join the Tricksters one must complete the entrance 
quest and survive, which most likely happened in your
case since you are here, able to read this book. If 
you wish to become a Trickster apply for membership
and follow the Jamadars instructions. If you happen to
pass the tests and survive the dark rituals, mayhaps 
shall Kina let you join this secret society.
