There are three tour-level quests in SaMorgan:

1. A girl (Lynet) needs an outfit for her job. She lists it when you
ask for a task. The adventurer can buy everything at Arinia's dress
shop. The list is random per player per login session. The location is
slightly randomized per reboot.

NPC: /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/npc/lynet.c
LOCATION (one of): /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/room/house0[34]*
SHOP: /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/room/dshop.c
ITEMS:
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/ftunic.c
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/fbelt.c
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/boots2.c

Reward: a valuable gem + 450XP.

2. A boy (Fearghal) needs a set of armours and weapons for his patrol
duty. The equipment can be bought at the SaMorgan shops or provided,
although they must be mediocre and non-magical. The location is
slightly randomized per reboot.

NPC: /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/npc/fearghal.c
LOCATION (one of): /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/room/house0[12]*
SHOP: /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/room/wshop.c
ITEMS (can be replaced with ones with similar name/quality):
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/wep/rapier.c
SHOP: /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/room/ashop.c
ITEMS (can be replaced with ones with similar name/quality):
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/mail.c
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/shield.c
  /d/Khalakhor/sw_speir/samorgan/arm/helm.c

Reward: a valuable gem + 450XP.

3. A lieutenant at the board room needs someone to go around four
guard towers of Sa Morgan and buy food and drinks for the guards and
the sergeants. Each sergeant and each guardroom make their own random
order. The food is from Sa Morgan's tavern.

NPC:
