Quest: Cadu baits

Description:
  A fisherman stands on the Cadu pier asking the player to retrieve some baits.
  He asks for one of three types of bait, randomized for the player with NAME_TO_RANDOM.
  He wants 5 baits, of one needs to be big to require some more time to find what's needed.
  The different types of baits are located:
   - Searchable in trees in forest north of Port Macdunn
   - Searchable in trees in the forst of Eil Galaith
   - Leftovers/droped during battle from the grugg on the island Carisca
