                           THE RHIAN OF ACCEPTANCE
                         of an Aspirant to the Order
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose:
   The Rhian of Acceptance is the rite by which those in darkness are
   chosen as aspirants to the Order. This simple rhian witnesses the
   Candidate's declaration of his intent to enter the Order and submit
   to the obedience of the Way, and to commission him to seek the Wisdom
   of Ossian as proof of his intentions.
 
Preparations:
   - Roles:
     - Presider:  Searmanagh or above
     - Defendant: Searmanagh or above, one who "presents" the Candidate.
     - Candidate: approved by the Leigh Orduigh.
   - Equipment needed: Vial of Holy Water, Aspirant's Shamrock Pendant.
   - Location: at the altar of a kirk.
   - Setup: Presider should have the vial and pendant and be at the
            altar. Defendant leads the Candidate to the altar.
 
 
 
Introduction:
  Pr:  Luaidh bi Araugh! (1)
  Pr:  Luaidh bi Maighal an Archangel! (2)
  Pr:  Luaidh bi am Solsheyderi! (3)
  Def: Seadh Car Daonnan! (4)
 
Interrogation:
  Pr:  Seeker, do you come before the altar of Ardaugh seeking the
       Harmony of The Way? (5)
  Can: I do
  Pr:  Should you be accepted to travel The Way, are you prepared to
       submit yourself in Obedience as a disciple of The Way? (6)
  Can: I am
  Pr:  So be it! (7)
 
Commissioning:
  Pr:  Hence you are sent on a journey into the mysteries of Ardaugh,
       to prove yourself worthy of the gift of Harmony. (8)
  Pr:  Seek ye the Wisdom of Ossian, the ancient Oracle of The Way,
       and you shall be enlightened. (9)
  Pr:  Receive this token of your journey. (10)
 
  Presider gives pendant to Candidate
  Pr:  This shall be a sign unto us, and to the world, that you seek
       the Harmony of Ardaugh. (11)
  Pr:  It shall also be a sign of your worthiness and enlightenment. (12)
 
Final Instructions and blessing.
  Pr:  Return it unto us when you are fully enligtened in he Wisdom of
       Ossian. (13)
  Pr:  And present it as your sacrifice and token of worthiness. (14)
 
  Presider sprinkles Candidate with Holy Water
 
  Pr:  May the Solsheyderi bless you on your quest. (15)
 
  Presider srsigns the Candidate.
 
  Pr:  Go in Harmony! (16)
 
  Defendant leads Candidate away from the altar.
  Defendant explains to the Candidate that he must complete the quest for
  Wisdom in the Revelation of Ossian, and that he shall receive little or
  no help from the Brathairs and Sistairs. He explains that the pendant
  will stay with him until it is ready to be presented.
