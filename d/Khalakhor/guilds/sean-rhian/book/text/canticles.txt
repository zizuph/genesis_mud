-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Argante
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Argante!
The seer of all mysteries!
Who has been given power over the living!
May she conform our hearts to Harmony!
And transform our minds with her Widsom!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Rhiannon
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Rhiannon!
Princess of the clouds!
Who rules the skies and all that is in them!
May she take us in flight into Glory!
The Glory of The Most High!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Kesair
-----------------------------------------------
Praised by Solsheyder Kesair!
Who brings forth healing waters from the earth!
And is given dominion over the fruits thereof!
May she heal our souls so that she
May present us purified before the Most High!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Irgalach
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Irgalach!
Defender of the Faith!
Who crushes the heads of the evil ones!
May he bring harmony to the children of Ardaugh!
May he bring peace unto the world!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Seanchan Tor
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Seanchan Tor!
High Priest of the Most High!
May he bring our offerings and praise
To the heavenly altar of Ardaugh!
 
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Camulos
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Camulos!
Who traverses the earth and sky and seas!
May he guide and protect us in our journeys!
And enchant the evil ones who would harm us!
And bring us safely into the Glory of Ardaugh!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Ambisargus
-------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Ambisargus!
He who is given all Knowledge of the earth!
May he impart to us his Wisdom
That we may bring forth Order from Chaos!
And destroy the darkness of ignorance of Ardaugh!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Sechnasach
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Sechnasach!
Who rules over the elements of the Earth!
May he forge our hearts in Harmony!
May he forge our bodies in Strength!
May he forge our minds in Wisdom!
 
-----------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------
Oran of Praise to Ragallach
-----------------------------------------------
Praised be Solsheyder Ragallach!
Who brings the purifying fires upon the earth!
Who proclaims to all the Holy Law of Ardaugh!
May our ears hear his words!
May our hearts be purified in this fire!
