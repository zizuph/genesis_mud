-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                  VESTMENTS OF THE ORDER OF SEAN-RHIAN
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
VESTMENTS -
   Alb          - a cowled white robe
   Cassock      - robe which fits tightly over the torso/arms with buttons
                  from neck to bottom down the front. (symbol of Searmanagh)
   Chausable    - large circlular, sleeeveless, cloak with a "hole" in the
                  center; head goes through hole, draping over the arms, front
                  and back (like a pauncho). (symbol of Saggart)
   Cope         - cape with a cowl (optional; searmanaghs use for cowling)
   Dalmatic     - short robe worn over an alb, coming down below the waste,
                  with sleeves coming down below the elbow. (symbol of Diaghin)
   Cincture     - a braided rope belt, ends hang down below knees
   Sash         - wide rectangular cloth belt, ends hang down to knees
   Mitre        - tall headgear made of two arched pieces ("pope hat")
   Scapular     - narrow, sleeveless cloak, consisting of two rectangular
                  pieces which hang from neck to ankles, one down the
                  front and another down the back. Manachs (NPC) only.
   Stole        - long rectangular cloth worn around back of neck and
                  hanging down both sides of the front below the knees.
   Surplice     - thin, see-through (of lace) robe; length is just below
                  waste, sleeves just below elbow. Worn over alb
   Tonsure      - round skullcap, indicates highest level of order
