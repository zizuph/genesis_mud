-------------------------------------------------------------------------
                  TITLES OF THE ORDER OF SEAN-RHIAN
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Promotions to each level are done by the Ard-Presbytair of each kirk,
though a Prebytair is capbable of performing the ceremony with permission,
while appointments to Ard-Presbytair and Seirsanach are made by the Oracle.
 
   TITLE             DESCRIPTION                           VESTMENT

   Solsheyder        - Apostle/Saint (historical title only)
   Oracle            - Guild Master

   Ard-Presbytair    - Leader of a Kirk                    purple mitre
   Presbytair        - Elder of a Kirk                     white tonsure
