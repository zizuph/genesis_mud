Chapter Three - XXX

     In time, the mysterious Syl-Custos flourished. On the 
remote island of Volere, they communed with the island
spirits, the Sylli. In their shrine, they perfected the arts
of summonry and geomancy. Little was known of the Syl-Custos
on the mainland of Khalakhor, and the Order attained an
almost mythical air. The reputation of Rydian grew in his 
whale-borne trips to the mainland, and he came to be known 
widely as Rydian the Caller.
      The Age of Rebirth, a time of relative peace for the 
fledgling order, eventually drew to a close. The Age of Legend
that followed was a time of terrible wars throughout the land
of Khalakhor. In time, an alliance consisting of some sixteen
magical and preistly orders was formed to repel the invading
goblin armies of the north. The members of the alliance came
to be known collectively as The Wise. The Syl-Custos were one
of the last member orders to enter into the circle of The
Wise. The Wise, shielded by ranks of elven armies, finally 
succeeded in driving out the invading forces of Chaos and the 
goblin armies. The elven armies suffered enormous losses in 
the battles, taking the brunt of the attacks from the north.
