A scroll for a control spell.

Properties:
Weight: 150gr
Volume: 100ml
Value: magical
Scroll

Adjectives:
thin old

Items: writing

Actions:
adds a cast action
In case of success an npc or a player comes under the control.
If you killed the goblin elder, the scroll will crumble in your hands
unless you wash his blood from your hands.

If you stole it, it will work for you.

The control drains mana permanently.
The controlled player/npc tries to revolt permanently.
