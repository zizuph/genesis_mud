Final vision:

1. Summary
A rich area specializing in players avg. 50-125. Up to 40
quests. Economic activities. Minor war setup.

All races and all alignments are welcome. Built-in hostility between
goblins and humans+elves. Neutrality for three other races.

Expand Mariners to allow to sail between parts of the domain. Add
Mariners chapter in Baile Ashlagh

Reorganize Genesis ShipLines

2. Areas

A. Summary

* SE Speir (humans, Port Macdunn+)
* SW Speir (elves, Sa Morgan+)
* N  Speir (goblins, Daenbraigh+)
* Nexus, war area
* Isles
* Expanded Cadu

The three speirs must be connected on foot

B. SE Speir

* Port Macdunn
* Baile Ashlagh
* Tabor Sogh
* Economy activities on the plains
* Distillers' (C) Guild in the Northwestern area
* Hammerhands (L) Guild on the SE/N speir border by the sea
* Blademasters (L) Guild
* More villages and military camps as killing grounds as well as
  quests
* Potential mobilization from the camps
* Rich wildlife

C. SW Speir

* Sa Morgan
* Another town
* Economy activities on the plains
* Ranger/Druid (O) guild close to N Speir border
* Clans (CLUB conversion) guild
* More villages and military camps as killing grounds as well as
  quests
* Potential mobilization from the camps
* Rich wildlife

D. N Speir

* Daenbraigh
* Another town
* Economy activities in the hills/mountains
* Mountaineers (C) guild
* Access to Hammerhands
* More villages and military camps as killing grounds as well as
  quests
* Potential mobilization from the camps

E. Nexus

* Three major campgrounds from each faction
* In the very middle mere with most dangerous creatures with rich
  rewards
* Ability to move between speirs through mountains bypassing the
  armies and the mere (high fatigue)

F. Isles

* Baile Ashlagh (q.v.) - Mariners Chapter
* Eil Galaith (q.v.) - Eil Galaith (R) guild
* Leprechaun Isle
  - Leprechaun (R) guild for Gnomes and Dwarves
  - A couple of villages
  - A few quests
  - A reason to return

G. Cadu

* Illuminators/Exploders (CLO) Guild for gnomes, humans and kenders
* Improve casino, add quests there (in the fashion similar to darts)
* Taiga forest for the biggest players
* Expand kobold and troll caves for 40-80 avg. players
