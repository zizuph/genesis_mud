Group:      1

Bit:        0

Name:       Imladris - Find Celeia's lost bracelet

Programmed: Finwe, 1998

Problem:    An elf warrior in Imladris, Celeia, has lost his 
            family brooch. Celeia spawns at 
            ~shire/rivendell/room/valley/v_path10 but wanders
            in this region.

Solution:   The brooch is in one of six rooms in the forest. 
            Find and return it to the npc. Rooms are:
            ~shire/room/forest/forest01, 
            ~shire/room/forest/forest12,
            ~shire/room/forest/forest33,
            ~shire/room/forest/forest58,
            ~shire/room/forest/forest64,
            ~shire/room/forest/forest83

Skills:     none

Risks:      none

XP-Scale:   1,000

Remarks:    The forest doesn't show any exits unless the player 
            has completed Elrond's quest, or is a Noldor elf.
