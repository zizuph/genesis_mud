Group:      2

Bit:        8

Name:       Get the Hobbit's Ball From the Boar

Programmed: Dunstable, 11/28/95

Problem:    Brunhilda the hobbit has lost her colored ball. Find 
            it and return it.

Solution:   Find the boar wandering in the Shire, and tickle his 
            chin. He will drop the ball. Be sure it is the same 
            color as Brunhilda specified.

Skills:     None

Risks:      None

XP-Scale:   500

Remarks:    This is a touristing quest. The boar does wander.
