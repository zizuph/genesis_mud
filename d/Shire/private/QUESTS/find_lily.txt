Name:       Find Lilies for Tom Bombadil

Programmed: Finwe, January 2002

Problem:    Tom Bombadil needs a new lily for his Goldberry. Go 
            and find a new one for her. 

Solution:   Go down to the Withywindle and search the river for 
            the flowers. Pick then and return to Tom with the flowers.

Skills:     None needed

Risks:      None needed

XP-Scale:   500

Remarks:    This is a simple quest, almost a tour.
