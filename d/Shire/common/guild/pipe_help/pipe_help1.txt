
These commands are added by the pipe of the Shires:

 adore Nob Nar            Start to sing for Nob Nar.
 recite <number>          Sing a merry song. There are 9 of them.
 shuffle [player]         Do a fancy dance (with a player).
 wigg    [player]         Make a magic gesture (at a player).
 barf    [player]         Puke all over (someone).
 bonk    <player>         Bonk a player on the head with your pipe.
 message <player> <text>  Send a message to one guild member. [msg]
 emessage <pl> <feeling>  Send a feeling to one guild member. [emsg]
 shon                     Turn the Shire-line  on.
 shoff                    Turn the Shire-line  off.
 shirelist                Print a list of all active guild members. [sl]
 smoke <tobacco>          Smoke some tobacco for pleasure.
 smell the air            Try to smell the scent of tobacco in a room.
 polish pipe              Polish your pipe.
 break pipe               Destruct the pipe.
 leave guild              Free yourself of the Hin Warrior Guild.
