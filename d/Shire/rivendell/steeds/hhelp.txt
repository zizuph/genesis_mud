  Commands available when leading your horse:
  ------------------------------------------
    unlead horse   Release a horse and leave it alone.
    mount horse    Unlead horse and then get on the horse
    hhome          Send your horse back to Imladris.
    hdrink         Make your horse drink.
    hgallop on     Make your horse gallop.
    hgallop off    Make your horse walk.
    automount      Enable/disable automount function.
    autodismount   Enable/disable autodismount function.
    hnice on/off   Make your horse react when people touch it.

  Commands available when riding your horse:
  -----------------------------------------
    dismount       Dismount your horse.
    hgallop on     Make your horse gallop.
    hgallop off    Make your horse walk.
    automount      Enable/disable automount function.
    autodismount   Enable/disable autodismount function.
    hnice on/off   Make your horse react when people touch it.
    hdrink         Make your horse drink. (hrdink from river etc.)
    hboard         Buy a ticket for you and your horse and board ship.

  Emotes available for a rider or leader of a horse
  ---------------------------------------

    Those emotes marked '*' may be used with adverbs.

    hsnort         Make your horse snort.
   *hscrape        Make your horse scrape its hooves through the ground.
   *hnuzzle who    Make your horse nuzzle someone.
