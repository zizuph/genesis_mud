                       AA move-behind descriptors:
                       ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

                   Submitted by Gorboth, February 2018

If target is not AA, or is of lower AA rank:

  ACTOR SEES:
    "You haul X forward and kick them roughly to the front of the action!"
  TARGET SEES:
    "X grabs you and hauls you in front of HIM. With a rough kick"
    " in your back, you are launched to the front of the action!"
  OTHERS SEE:
    "X turns and hauls Y forward. With a rough kick to the backside,"
    " X is propelled to the front of the action!"

If target is AA of higher rank:

  ACTOR SEES:
    "Taking too much heat, you cry out to X and then move aside as they"
    " rush to the front of the action!"
  TARGET SEES:
    "X cries out to you, taking too much heat. SHE falls back as you"
    " rush to the front of the action!"
  OTHERS SEE:
    "X, taking too much heat, cries out to Y. SHE falls back as Y"
    " rushes to the front of the action!"
