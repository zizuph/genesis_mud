Mariner
While there is a lot you can explore on foot in Krynn, there is a lot that can only be accessed by sea!  In the port city of Kalaman, one may train their skills as a craftsman mariner, chartering personal ships to sail the high seas!

Sail the Courrain Ocean around the peninsula of Nordmaar to the Bloodsea of Istar! Engage in sea battles against pirates, corsairs, slavers, and even Dragonarmy warships! Search for hidden treasure, and explore underwater ruins! Make port at the minotaur isle of Mithas, even sail to the haunted Isle of Karthay. Adventure abounds, and the seas hide many things fell and fantastic!
