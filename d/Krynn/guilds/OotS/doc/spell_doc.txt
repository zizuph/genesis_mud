Holy Order of the Stars spell doc.

All spells can be found in /d/Krynn/guilds/OotS/spells/
All spell objects in /d/Krynn/guilds/OotS/spells/objs/

GENERAL SPELL RESTRICTIONS:
If caster alignment drops below 600, access to all spells removed.

Combat aid of spells modified downwards as alignment drops below 1000
by a factor of alignment / 1000.

There is only a 10% casting success chance if caster in not wearing the 
medallion of faith.

LEVEL 9 SPELLS (Cleric)
===========================================================================

Spell name:      osmite                    Element:      Life (40)
Spell effect:    mass harm spell           Form:         Abjuration (30)
Type:            instant                   Difficulty:   HARD
Target:          undeads                   Combat aid:   up to 50
Spell power:     Class 3                   Mana cost:    Special

Peaceful:        No                        Stationary:   Yes
Verbal:          Yes                       Somatic:      Yes

Casting time:    2.0x, 8s 
Components:      holy water (46cc)

TAX:             1% (magical layman class 3 spell)

Comments:
Mass harm spell against up to 5 creatures with the LIVE_I_UNDEAD prop, with
depreciating damage after the first three. As with the standard mass harm
spell, mana cost is dependent on the number of targets.

caid of 150.

Caid is reduced by 15 if omend or oheroism active.
