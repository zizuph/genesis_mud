STEPS TO FOLLOW WHEN ADDING NEW SPELLS:

1. Code spell based on spell templates at
/d/Genesis/specials/std/spells/

2. In ~guilds/sohm/remove_all_spells.h in the
remove_all_spells function add:
unmemorise_spell and 
unsubscribe_spell_from_spellbook

This will ensure the spell is removed when they leave the 
guild (there is an exception to this for when they switch
from SoHM to WoHS).

3. In ~guilds/sohm/souls/common_spell_soul.c add spell to:
query_cmdlist()
query_ability_map()
query_sohm_spell_level

4. Add spell to spellbook at 
~guilds/sohm/objs/spellbook_spells.h
